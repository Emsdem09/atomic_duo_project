Our Story

Ever since 1999, Flat Cap Coffee has been the number 1 coffee cart in the UK.

All of our coffee beans are hand picked in Guatemala, within the high

planes of Fraijanes where the climate is warm and the soil potassium-rich.

The beans are then roasted, giving a full-bodied, acidic and soft aroma to the coffee.

Flat Cap Coffee guarantees an explosion of flavours on your palete that will brighten up your day!
